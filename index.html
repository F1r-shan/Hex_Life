Hex Life

A living world in your browser

How to Play

  1. ▶ Start — opens the simulation world.
  2. Generate — type any seed and click Generate (or press Enter) to create a new world. Leave blank for a random one.
  3. + Add Human — click the button, then click any green/sand/rock hex to place people. Click an occupied hex to remove them. Use the number box to place up to 50 at once.
  4. Watch them build settlements, fall in love, have children, form alliances, and go to war — all on their own.
